## SMS Best Practices

### Character Limits
- Keep messages under 160 characters when possible
- Use URL shorteners for links
- Abbreviate when necessary (but maintain clarity)

### Timing
- Send during business hours (9 AM - 8 PM local time)
- Avoid sending on weekends unless urgent
- Respect user timezone preferences

### Opt-Out
- Include opt-out instructions: "Reply STOP to unsubscribe"
- Honor opt-out requests immediately
- Provide opt-in confirmation

### Personalization
- Use recipient's name when possible
- Include relevant context (company name, dates, etc.)
- Make messages actionable

### Compliance
- Comply with TCPA (Telephone Consumer Protection Act)
- Require explicit opt-in for SMS
- Provide clear opt-out instructions
- Store consent records

---

**All SMS templates are concise, actionable, and compliant with SMS regulations.**
