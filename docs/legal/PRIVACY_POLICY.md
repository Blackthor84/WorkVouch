# PeerCV Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## 1. Introduction

PeerCV ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email address, password, full name
- **Profile Information**: Job history, work experience, skills, certifications
- **Industry Information**: Selected industry (Law Enforcement, Security, Hospitality, Retail)
- **Contact Information**: Phone number (optional, for SMS verification)
- **Payment Information**: Processed securely through Stripe (we do not store full payment details)
- **References**: Peer references you write or receive
- **Messages**: Direct messages sent through the platform

### 2.2 Information Automatically Collected
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, features used, time spent
- **Device Information**: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system
- **Cookies**: Authentication cookies, session cookies, analytics cookies
- **Log Data**: Server logs, error logs, access timestamps

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties
- **Supabase**: Authentication data, database records
- **Stripe**: Payment transaction data, subscription status
- **Coworker Verification**: Information from other users who verify your employment

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Service Provision
- Create and manage your account
- Display your profile to employers and other users
- Match you with coworkers based on job history
- Calculate and display your Trust Score
- Process payments and manage subscriptions
- Send you notifications about references, matches, and updates

### 3.2 Communication
- Send welcome emails and onboarding messages
- Notify you of coworker verification requests
- Alert you to new references and Trust Score changes
- Send security alerts and account updates
- Respond to your support requests

### 3.3 Improvement and Analytics
- Analyze usage patterns to improve the Service
- Conduct research and development
- Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
- Ensure platform security and stability

### 3.4 Legal Compliance
- Comply with applicable laws and regulations
- Respond to legal requests and court orders
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Protect our rights and the rights of users

## 4. Where Your Data Is Stored

### 4.1 Primary Storage
- **Supabase (PostgreSQL)**: All user data, profiles, job history, references, messages
- **Supabase Storage**: Profile photos and uploaded documents
- **Supabase Auth**: Authentication credentials and session data

### 4.2 Third-Party Services
- **Stripe**: Payment and subscription data
- **Email Service Provider**: Email delivery and tracking
- **SMS Provider**: SMS delivery (if phone verification is enabled)

### 4.3 Data Location
- Data is stored in Supabase's secure data centers
- Data may be stored in multiple geographic locations for redundancy
- We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest

## 5. Data Retention

### 5.1 Active Accounts
- We retain your data for as long as your account is active
- Active accounts are those that have been accessed within the past 12 months

### 5.2 Deleted Accounts
- Upon account deletion, most data is removed within 30 days
- Some data may be retained for up to 90 days for security and legal purposes
- References you wrote may remain visible to recipients (anonymized)
- Trust Score history may be retained in aggregated, anonymized form

### 5.3 Legal Requirements
- We may retain data longer if required by law
- We may retain data to resolve disputes or enforce agreements
- Financial records are retained as required by tax and accounting laws

## 6. Your Privacy Rights

### 6.1 GDPR Rights (European Users)
- **Right to Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Right to Rectification**: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Right to Erasure**: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Right to Restrict Processing**: Limit how we use your data
- **Right to Data Portability**: Receive your data in a portable format
- **Right to Object**: Object to certain types of data processing

### 6.2 CCPA Rights (California Users)
- **Right to Know**: Request information about data collection and use
- **Right to Delete**: Request deletion of your personal information
- **Right to Opt-Out**: Opt out of the sale of personal information (PeerCV does not sell personal information)
- **Right to Non-Discrimination**: Exercise your rights without discrimination

### 6.3 How to Exercise Your Rights
- Contact us at privacy@peercv.com
- Include your account email and specific request
- We will respond within 30 days (or as required by law)
- We may require identity verification for security

## 7. Cookie Usage

### 7.1 Types of Cookies
- **Authentication Cookies**: Required for login and session management
- **Session Cookies**: Maintain your session while using the Service
- **Analytics Cookies**: Help us understand how users interact with the Service
- **Preference Cookies**: Remember your settings and preferences

### 7.2 Cookie Management
- You can control cookies through your browser settings
- Disabling cookies may limit Service functionality
- Required cookies cannot be disabled without affecting Service access

## 8. Employer Access to Data

### 8.1 What Employers Can See
- Public profile information (name, industry, Trust Score)
- Verified job history (company, dates, title)
- Peer references (with your consent)
- Skills and certifications

### 8.2 What Employers Cannot See
- Your email address or contact information (unless you share it)
- Private messages between you and other users
- Payment or subscription information
- Internal account settings

### 8.3 Employer Subscriptions
- Employers with active subscriptions can view full candidate profiles
- Employer access is logged and tracked
- You will be notified when an employer views your profile (if enabled)

## 9. SMS and Email Verification Data

### 9.1 Email Verification
- We collect your email address for account creation
- We send verification emails to confirm your email address
- Email addresses are stored securely and used only for Service-related communication

### 9.2 SMS Verification (Optional)
- Phone numbers are collected only if you opt in to SMS notifications
- Phone numbers are stored securely and used only for verification and notifications
- You can opt out of SMS at any time through your account settings

## 10. Data Security

### 10.1 Security Measures
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encryption at rest for sensitive data
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access controls and authentication requirements
- Secure password hashing (bcrypt)

### 10.2 Data Breach Notification
- We will notify affected users within 72 hours of discovering a breach
- Notifications will include details about what data was compromised
- We will take immediate steps to mitigate the breach

## 11. Children's Privacy

- PeerCV is not intended for users under 18 years of age
- We do not knowingly collect information from children
- If we discover that we have collected information from a child, we will delete it immediately
- Parents or guardians who believe we have collected information from a child should contact us

## 12. International Data Transfers

- Your data may be transferred to and stored in countries other than your own
- We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers
- By using PeerCV, you consent to the transfer of your data as described in this policy

- We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third parties
- We encourage you to read the privacy policies of third-party services
