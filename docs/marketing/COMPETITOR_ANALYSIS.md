# PeerCV Competitor Analysis

**Comprehensive Competitive Landscape Analysis**

## Competitors Overview

### 1. LinkedIn

**What They Do**: Professional networking and job search platform

**Strengths**:
- Massive user base (900M+ users)
- Established brand recognition
- Comprehensive job search features
- Professional networking tools
- Company pages and job postings

**Weaknesses**:
- **No verification system** - Anyone can claim any job
- References are informal (recommendations, not verified)
- No Trust Score or verification metrics
- Fake profiles and inflated credentials common
- Not industry-specific

**PeerCV Advantage**:
- **Peer verification required** - Can't fake jobs
- **Trust Score system** - Quantified trustworthiness
- **Industry-specific** - Tailored for law enforcement, security, hospitality, retail
- **Verified references** - Only from actual coworkers
- **Instant employer verification** - No waiting weeks
