# WorkVouch Pricing Strategy & Revenue Projections
## Investor One-Page Summary

---

## Executive Summary

WorkVouch is a peer-verified work history platform that creates trust between workers and employers. Our pricing strategy targets two primary revenue streams: **employee subscriptions** and **employer subscriptions**, with a specialized focus on the **security industry**.

---

## Pricing Tiers & Revenue Model

### Employee/Worker Revenue Stream

| Tier | Price | Key Features | Target Market |
|------|-------|-------------|---------------|
| **Free** | $0/month | 3 references, basic trust score | Market entry, user acquisition |
| **Pro Worker** | $4.99/month | Unlimited references, verified badge, priority ranking | Active job seekers (estimated 20% conversion) |
| **Trust Boost** | $9.99 one-time | Instant credibility boost | Job seekers needing immediate visibility |

**Revenue Projection (Year 1):**
- 10,000 free users → 2,000 Pro Worker subscribers (20% conversion)
- 2,000 × $4.99 × 12 months = **$119,760/year**
- 1,000 Trust Boost purchases = **$9,990/year**
- **Total Employee Revenue: ~$130,000/year**

---

### Employer Revenue Stream

| Tier | Price | Monthly Limits | Target Market |
|------|-------|----------------|---------------|
| **Starter** | $49/month | 15 searches, 10 reports | Small businesses, startups |
| **Team** | $149/month | 50 searches, 40 reports | Growing HR departments (most popular) |
| **Pro** | $299/month | 150 searches, 120 reports | Established companies |
| **Enterprise** | $499/month | Unlimited | Large organizations |
| **Pay-Per-Use** | $14.99/report | Per report | Occasional users |
| **Security Bundle** | $199/month | 80 reports, unlimited searches | Security agencies (niche focus) |

**Revenue Projection (Year 1):**
- 50 Starter subscribers: 50 × $49 × 12 = $29,400
- 100 Team subscribers: 100 × $149 × 12 = $178,800
- 30 Pro subscribers: 30 × $299 × 12 = $107,640
- 10 Enterprise subscribers: 10 × $499 × 12 = $59,880
- 200 Pay-Per-Use reports/month: 200 × $14.99 × 12 = $35,976
- 50 Security Bundle subscribers: 50 × $199 × 12 = $119,400
- **Total Employer Revenue: ~$531,096/year**

## Market Strategy: Security Industry Focus

### Why Security?

1. **High Verification Needs** — Security agencies require verified guard history
2. **Regulatory Compliance** — License and certificate tracking essential
3. **High Turnover** — Constant hiring creates recurring revenue
4. **Premium Pricing** — Security Bundle at $199/month (higher margin)

### Security Industry Opportunity

- **Target Market:** 10,000+ security agencies in US
- **Penetration Goal:** 1% (100 agencies) in Year 1
- **Revenue Potential:** 100 × $199 × 12 = **$238,800/year** (from security alone)

---

## Competitive Advantages

1. **Peer Verification** — Unique model creates trust through coworker confirmation
2. **Trust Score** — Quantifiable credibility metric
3. **Industry Focus** — Specialized features for security agencies
4. **Two-Sided Market** — Network effects as more users join
5. **Portable Records** — Workers own their verified history

---

## Growth Strategy

### Phase 1 (Months 1-6): Security Industry Focus
- Target 50 security agency customers
- Build specialized features (license tracking, shift tools)
- Establish credibility in security niche

### Phase 2 (Months 7-12): Expand to Other Industries
- Healthcare, law enforcement, hospitality, retail, warehouse
- Leverage security success stories

- Integration with ATS systems
