## Location Data

WorkVouch displays approximate geographic adoption to help users and employers understand platform reach. We collect and display only **coarse, aggregated location data** (country and U.S. state level).

We **do not** collect or store precise locations, GPS coordinates, city-level data, or real-time movement. All location data is shown in aggregate to protect individual privacy.

# App Store / Google Play — Data Safety Declaration

**Data type:** Approximate location (coarse)

**Usage:** Analytics, platform insight

**Not used for:** Tracking, advertising, surveillance

**Retention:** Aggregated only, non-identifiable
